Pune Institute of Computer Technology commonly known as PICT is an independent private engineering college in Dhankawadi, Pune, India. The Society for Computer Technology and Research, SCTR, found this institute in 1983. It has always been recognized as a leader in information technology education and research. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) of India both accredited PICT.

PICT Pune is awarded various rankings such as 201-250 in Engineering ranking by NIRF (2021), 201-250 in Engineering ranking by NIRF (2020), and 73rd top engineering colleges in India by The Week (2019), etc.

The institution is well-known for its undergraduate and graduate programmes in Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. Electronics and communications engineering in Ph.D. degrees are also available.

PICT Pune features a vibrant environment and campus life, which includes athletics, extracurricular activities, etc. The various departments at PICT Pune are Computer Science, Electronics & Telecommunication, and Information Technology.

Interview rooms, student coordination rooms, and firm operational rooms are all available in PICT's training and placement cells. In 2020, PITC placed 514 students, had 115 recruiters, and had an overall average salary package of INR 7.6 LPA.

Among private institutions in the state of Maharashtra, this was the first to provide a Computer Engineering branch. PICT features well-equipped laboratories with the latest technology, as well as highly skilled teaching professionals in all disciplines.

PICT Pune Admission Process

PICT considers the MHT-CET and JEE-Main marks of candidates for admissions. Students who have successfully completed a diploma from a polytechnic institute are also eligible for admission to direct second year. It is regarded as one of the most selective institutes. There are other programmes in college for students who are non-residential Indians (NRIs).

PICT UG Admission process

  • Fill out and complete the online PICT registration form.
  • Attempt either the JEE Main or the MHT CET test.
  • To get selected, you must have to clear the PICT B.Tech cutoff.
  • Shortlisted candidates must then enroll in the counselling process.
  • They must complete the PICT admissions process and pay the money after receiving their seat reservation.

PICT PG Admission process

  • Fill out the PICT online registration form.
  • Attend and pass the GATE entrance test.
  • Candidates must fulfill the M.Tech. cutoff requirement.
  • Shortlisted students must participate in the PICT M.Tech counselling procedure.
  • Following seat reservation, complete the paperwork.

PICT Ph.D. Admission process

  • The applicant must complete the Ph.D. registration form.
  • The merit list may be seen on the official website.
  • Applicants must participate in the counselling process.
  • Finally, applicants must confirm their documents and pay the fees.
